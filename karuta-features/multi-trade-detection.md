# Multi-Trade Detection

Be cautious of a recent scam. Scammers are offering to purchase all your bits in one go by providing pre-written text for trades, but they secretly add tickets to the transaction. To avoid being tricked, **please** type out your own trade details instead of copying and pasting text provided by others.
